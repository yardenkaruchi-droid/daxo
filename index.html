03 — Product

An instrument for physical intelligence.

d4 · evo · 01

Engineered past the human form.

More independent joints than a human hand, with redundant tendons on every finger. A geometry not constrained by anatomy — tuned for what real factories and labs need to do.

  • Active degrees of freedom24+ DoF
  • Redundant tendons / finger3×
  • Closed-loop control rate1 kHz
  • Mean time before service10,000 h
d4 · evo · 02

Soft tendons. Hard precision.

A tendon-driven architecture transfers torque without the rigidity of conventional grippers — human-scale compliance, sub-millimeter placement.

  • Fingertip repeatability±0.4 mm
  • Sustained payload3 kg
  • Grasp force range0.2 – 60 N
  • Compliance modesVariable
d4 · evo · 03

One platform. Many tasks.

A modular fingertip and sensor stack covers a wider task envelope than a fleet of single-purpose tools. Swap end-effects in seconds. Train once, deploy across lines.

  • Modular tip variants6+ at launch
  • Tactile channels / finger32
  • InterfacesEtherCAT · ROS 2
